Welcome to Aim for Grade 9.

Try out this test to see how well you know GCSE algebra needed for achieving a Grade 9. It will show you what you're good at and what you need to work on. Then, it'll give you suggestions for studying those areas.

Is this test for you?

This test is for students who want to get a Grade 9 in GCSE Maths. Make sure you're at least at a Grade 7 level, or else it might be too hard for you and not very useful.

Instructions for completing the test:

  • Answer each question on paper.
  • Click "show answer" after finishing to check your answer. Then, choose if you got it right or wrong.
  • If a question seems too difficult, you can skip it. If you do this twice in a module, you'll be moved onto the next module.
  • If a question seems too easy, select to skip it, and we'll mark it as correct for you.
